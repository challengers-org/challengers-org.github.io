Our Activities

Our association offers a variety of activities focused on students and designed to meet their educational and developmental needs. We strive to create an environment that combines entertainment with education, providing a comprehensive educational program covering multiple fields such as mathematics, science, and arts, alongside recreational activities that enhance students' intellectual and social skills.

Below are the different activities organized by the association:

Green March and Glorious Independence Day Celebration - November 10, 2024

On Sunday, November 10, 2024, to commemorate the anniversary of the Green March and the Glorious Independence Day, a morning educational event was organized at the Ait Ali and Issa primary school center in partnership with the Challengers Association for Education and Culture. The event began with verses from the Holy Quran, followed by everyone standing for the national anthem. A welcome speech was delivered by the president of the Challengers Association, and the team of educational facilitators presented segments that included educational and awareness workshops, a clown performance, and games for students. Mothers of students also benefited from an awareness workshop on public health, and the event concluded with a reenactment of the Green March by students in the school courtyard.

Excellence Celebration - July 09, 2024

In partnership with the Challengers Association for Education and Culture, Ait Ali and Issa primary school organized an excellence celebration on Tuesday, July 09, 2024, under the theme "Encouraging Excellence as a Foundation for Academic Achievement." After the opening Quran recitation and national anthem, students presented various segments including songs and plays. The event honored outstanding primary students and seven high school graduates from the village, and also recognized a departing teacher from the school's educational staff.
