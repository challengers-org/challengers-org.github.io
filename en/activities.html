March 12, 2025
Quran Recitation and Memorization Competition

As part of educational and cultural activities aimed at enhancing religious values and developing students' skills in Quran recitation and memorization, Ait Ali o Issa School Group, in partnership with Challengers Association and Tandout School Activities Development Association, organized a Quran Recitation and Memorization Competition on Wednesday, March 12, 2025, at the institution's premises, in celebration of the blessed month of Ramadan.

Participating Institutions

The competition featured remarkable participation from students of several educational institutions, including:

  • Ait Ali o Issa School Group
  • Toundoute School Group
  • Aligh N Terga School Group
  • Tiflit School Group
  • Amakchoud School Group

Participants showed great enthusiasm, presenting melodious recitations and outstanding performances, making the competition highly competitive in an atmosphere of faith, brotherly competition, and continuous encouragement from the audience.

Competition Results

After a thorough evaluation by a judging panel consisting of esteemed teachers and experts in Tajweed and recitation rules, the results were as follows:

  • 1 First Place: Amakchoud School Group for their outstanding performance and mastery in recitation and Tajweed.
  • 2 Second Place: Tiflit School Group with impressive recitations that were well-received by all.
  • 3 Third Place: Toundoute School Group whose participants demonstrated a high level of proficiency in memorization and recitation.
Honoring the Winners and Appreciating the Participants

At the end of the event, awards and certificates of appreciation were distributed to the winners, in recognition of their efforts and to encourage them to continue their dedication to the Quran. The organizers also expressed their gratitude and appreciation to all participants, praising the high level they exhibited and wishing them continued success in their educational and religious journeys.

November 10, 2024
Green March and Glorious Independence Day Celebration

On Sunday, November 10, 2024, to commemorate the anniversary of the Green March and the Glorious Independence Day, a morning educational event was organized at the Ait Ali and Issa primary school center in partnership with the Challengers Association for Education and Culture. The event began with verses from the Holy Quran, followed by everyone standing for the national anthem. A welcome speech was delivered by the president of the Challengers Association, and the team of educational facilitators presented segments that included educational and awareness workshops, a clown performance, and games for students. Mothers of students also benefited from an awareness workshop on public health, and the event concluded with a reenactment of the Green March by students in the school courtyard.
